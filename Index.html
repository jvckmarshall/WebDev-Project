As this is my first showcase of my photography,
I decided to show some variety of work.

Turns out these pictures were the best I could come up with. Anyway, intro aside,
here is a set of portraits and abstract/nature shots to tickle your fancy.

My passion for photography has many roots, but the simplest is the aesthetic.

That's why I pay special attention to the small details, the eye enjoys the continuation of crisp lines and pleasing colours. Faces in portraits should reflect the feeling or theme the image conveys, to which posing and positioning plays a large part.

Camera

I am currently using the Sony a37 camera body, a small and versatile DLSR with an exceptional sensor and adequate MP..

Lenses

Out of all lenses I have in rotation, the most valuable to this showcase was my nifty 50mm, for its aperture adjustability. I also own a 75-300 zoom lens, a rokinon fisheye, and a standard 15-70 lens.

Accessories

My Lowepro Slingshot camera bag is great for holding all of my camera gear at once, but also keeping it accessible. I also own a CamCaddy, great for steady sweeping video clips or action shots.
